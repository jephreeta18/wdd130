White Water Rafting Company Site Plan

Designer: Vanessa Nuckolls

Overview

Purpose

To build revenues for the White Water Rafting Company.

Audience

  • Who are the target customers? Tourists and other adventure seekers, including families and those ranging from novice to intermediate rafters.
  • What questions will the website answer for them? The website will give them pricing tiers, safety information, booking information, requirements for rafting, and the like.
  • How will my audience access the site? They will most likely be accessing from a mobile device.
